Cloud traffic data near to you.

Had you ever thought about consulting traffic data to improve your traveller life?
Make your dreams come true with Orbit, and get traffic information around you to improve your day-to-day city trips. Never be late again!


Simple and flowed traffic.

Can city traffic be more fluid with the use of pressure sensors data? With our datasets and it's accordingly application, traffic can be redirected to improve the more dense streets flow and decrease the traffics jams inside the city.

Choose the best route.

Have you ever wondered if taking a different mean of transportation than your daily one would be faster to arrive to work that morning?
With Orbit you can see the traffic flow on the streets where your bus uses to go, and choose the best alternative way to get your destination.

Don't get stuck in an emergency.

Owner of some emergency transport services like ambulances, police cars or fire tracks?
Use the data provided by orbit to avoid traffic jams in emergency scenarios by choossing alternatives paths to get to your destination.
With Orbit and our cloud data, you mortality can be decreased by 10% in emergenciy situations like this.

What is it useful for?

Here are some of the advantatges of usig Orbit Traffic Data but possibilites are unlimited.

Real-Time Traffic

Orbit offers a tool to visualize the traffic flow. It's top-notch technology.


Traffic Parameters

Orbit provides traffic parameters like rush hours traffic and crowds.


Chronological Stadistics

Efficient roads and public transport design with the stadistics provided.

Mobility Profiles

Each road will have a different mobility profile depending on it's flow.

Traffic Monitoring

Specific design to monitor the logistic vehicles of an enterprise or organization.

Timed Routes

Calculation and analysis of the vehicles routes and it's routes' time.
